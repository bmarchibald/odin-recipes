Christmas Wassail

  • 1 c. sugar
  • 2 c. water
  • 12 oz. orange juice frozen concentrate
  • 5 c. lemonade
  • 2 qt. apple juice or cider
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 8 whole allspice berries
  • 10 whole cloves

Place sugar, water, orange juice, lemonade and apple juice in a large crockpot on low heat. Place spices in a steeping basket and drop in wassail mixture. Simmer all day. Remove spice basket and serve warm. Servings: 18.

Note: I received this recipe from Cheryl Smith of the Home Ranch Ward. As an added bonus, this wassail fills your house with a wonderful Christmas smell.
