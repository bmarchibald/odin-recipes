Grapefruit Slush

  • 2 c. water
  • 1/2 c. sugar
  • 1/4 c. maraschino cherries, juice
  • 12 oz. grapefruit sections
  • 48 oz. 7-Up

Boil water and sugar to make syrup. Add cherry juice and grapefruit. Pour into a 9 x 13-inch pan and freeze. Serve broken pieces in a small bowl with 7-Up over it and a cherry on top. Servings: 24.

Note: I received this recipe from Jeanne Mayberry.
