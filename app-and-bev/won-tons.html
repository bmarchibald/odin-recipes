Won Tons

  • 1 lb. ground pork, ground beef or shrimp
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 oz. water chestnuts, canned, chopped fine
  • 4 green onions, chopped
  • 3 stalks celery, chopped
  • 1 whole egg
  • 1 whole egg 40 won-ton wrappers

Brown meat with salt. Put meat in a bowl and add the rest of the ingredients and mix. Place about 1 teaspoon of filling in the won ton skin, fold over and seal with water. Deep-fry on both sides until brown. Serve with Sweet-and-Sour Sauce (see sauces). Servings: 40.

Note: I received this recipe from Amy. She received it from an Asian-American sister in her ward in Ammon. It's excellent and authentic.
