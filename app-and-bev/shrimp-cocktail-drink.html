Shrimp Cocktail Drink

  • 1 bunch celery
  • 6 qt. tomato juice (Campbell's)
  • 18 oz. shrimp, canned and broken
  • 20 oz. cocktail sauce (Nallys)
  • 3 T. lemon juice
  • 1 T. horseradish
  • 1 c. catsup (Heinz)

Separate and clean celery stalks. Cut into (24) 8-inch pieces. Mix remaining ingredients. Pour into glasses and garnish with piece of celery. Servings: 24.

Note: It really is good!
