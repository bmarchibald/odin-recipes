Shrimp Cocktail Platter

  • 8 oz. cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 lb. salad shrimp, drained
  • 12 oz. cocktail sauce (Del Monte)
  • 1 lb. crackers (Ritz preferred)

Press cream cheese onto serving platter. Chill. Mix drained shrimp and cocktail sauce. Spread over cream cheese. Arrange crackers around the outside of platter. Serve. Servings: 24.

Note: Matt, Dad and I all love this. Each New Years, Matt or I make this. It's really great, try it!
