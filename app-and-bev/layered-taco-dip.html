Layered Taco Dip

  • c 16 oz. refried beans
  • 10 oz. salsa
  • 1 c. mayonnaise
  • 1 c. sour cream
  • 3 whole avocados, diced
  • 1 tsp. lemon juice (opt.)
  • 1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce (opt.)
  • 1/4 c. chopped green onions
  • 1/2 c. chopped tomato
  • 2 c. grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 c. olives, sliced

Layer ingredients onto serving platter as follows: 1st Layer: Mix refried beans and salsa and spread on serving platter. 2nd Layer: Mix equal parts mayonnaise and sour cream. Spread over bean layer. 3rd Layer: Mix avocados, lemon juice and Worcestershire sauce. Add to previous layer. 4th Layer: Top with chopped onions, chopped tomatoes, grated cheese and sliced olives. Servings: 24

Note: I received this recipe from Trenna Hillam when she served as my counselor in the Primary Presidency. It's yummy!
